Our Mission

We are proud to present OTSS, a problem writing group aiming to create a biannual mock AMC series, one in the spring and one in the fall. This series of contests will be designed for people to get more familiar and obtain extra practice with the MAA series test (AMCs, AIME, and USA(J)MO). For our mock, we have called it the Test Mathematics Competitions (TMC), the Online Test Invitational Examination (OTIE), and the Online Test Junior Mathematical Olympiad (OTJMO).

Regarding the difficulty of our contests, we aim to create mock contests that are slightly more difficult that the official MAA series tests. However, we strive to moderate the difficulty level of our contests so that they are still approachable and within reach of official MAA series tests.
