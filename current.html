Introduction

Welcome to the Online Test Snowy Series, season two of the Online Test Seasonal Series. This year, we have made some changes regarding our running of our mock AMC series. This includes improving the efficiency and communication of our team members, better organizing our problems, and allocating more time to design the contests. We hope you enjoy this edition of the Online Test Seasonal Series!

Background of the Series:

This series of contests will once again be designed for people to get more familiar and obtain extra practice with the MAA series test, i.e. AMC, AIME, and USA(J)MO, and we have again called it the Test Mathematics Competitions (TMC), the Online Test Invitational Examination (OTIE), and the Online Test Junior Mathematical Olympiad (OTJMO), respectively. Please note that, unlike the MAA Tests, you do not have to qualify for a competition to take it, and you do not have to be under a certain age to take any of the tests.

Submissions:
  • Submissions for TMC and OTIE will be via private message to AoPS users DeToasty3, Emathmaster, jeteagle, and kevinmathz, who will add other members of the committee to the private message.
  • Grading will be automatic via our computer code; however, this does not mean we will be able to return scores immediately. Thus, in order for us to grade your submissions for the TMC and OTIE, please submit your solutions in one line.
  • If you wish to leave anything blank for the TMC, please write 0. For example, this would be a valid submission:
    0BCDE A0CDE AB0DE ABC0E ABCD0
  • If you wish to leave anything blank for the OTIE, please write BBB. For example, if the answers to their problems were their numbers, with problem 11 blank, this would be a valid submission:
    001 002 003 004 005 006 007 008 009 010 BBB 012 013 014 015
  • For the OTJMO, please consult the Instructions document (located in the "Contest Info and Test URLs" section of this post) for information on how to submit.
Aftermath:
